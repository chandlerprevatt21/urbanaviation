Our Mission

The Urban Aviation Group, LLC is dedicated to promoting diversity within the Aviation industry by providing education and support to youth in underserved communities. Our courses are thoughtfully designed to introduce students to various aspects of aviation and aerospace. Although we warmly welcome and encourage all students to participate, our program places a specific focus on fostering diversity in the aviation industry.
